\# Ecommerce SQL Analysis Project



\## Project Overview



This project focuses on analyzing ecommerce sales data using SQL and Power BI. The objective of the project is to extract meaningful business insights from sales transactions, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends.



The project demonstrates practical SQL querying skills, data analysis techniques, and dashboard visualization capabilities suitable for Data Analyst and Business Analyst roles.

\## SQL Analysis Performed



The following SQL analyses were performed in this project:



\### 1. Total Rows Count



Calculated the total number of records available in the dataset.



\### 2. Sales Data Preview



Displayed sample records for initial data understanding and validation.



\### 3. Total Sales Analysis



Calculated the overall sales generated from ecommerce transactions.



\### 4. Total Profit Analysis



Calculated the total profit earned across all orders.



\### 5. Category-wise Sales Analysis



Identified top-performing product categories based on sales.



\### 6. Region-wise Sales Analysis



Analyzed sales distribution across different regions.



\### 7. Payment Method Analysis



Determined the most frequently used payment methods.



\### 8. Customer Segment Analysis



Compared sales and profit across customer segments.



\### 9. Monthly Sales Trend



Analyzed monthly sales performance trends.



\### 10. Top Products by Sales



Identified top-selling products based on total sales.



\### 11. Delivery Status Analysis



Analyzed order delivery status distribution.



\### 12. Top Products by Profit



Identified products generating the highest profit.



\---



\## Key Insights



\* Grocery category generated the highest sales.

\* North region achieved the highest regional sales.

\* Debit Card was the most preferred payment method.

\* Home Office segment contributed the highest sales and profit.

\* Organic Rice emerged as the top-performing product by both sales and profit.

\* Monthly sales showed fluctuations with strong performance in October.
